I am Victor, a freelance digital artist from Belarus. I've always loved creating art, painting, and drawing ever since my childhood. Films and gaming have had a large influence on the pieces that I create, with a focus on sci-fi and post-apocalyptic themes.

Despite lacking formal artistic education, I am constantly striving to learn from others and improve my skills. I experiment with various styles such as realistic, cartoon, anime, and pixel art. Video games have held a special place in my heart since my childhood.

However, the year 2015 brought about a profound change in my life. It was then that I discovered the amazing world of Virtual Photography. As a long-time fan of both the beautiful and the creepiness of in-game worlds, I was filled with joy when I realized I could capture these moments through virtual photography.

If you're interested in commissioning artwork, hiring me as a screenshot artist for your game, or simply want to have a chat, please don't hesitate to reach out to me via email.
